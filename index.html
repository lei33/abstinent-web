On-device. Private. Yours.

Understand yourself, not compare yourself.

Abstinent is a recovery companion for iPhone — whether you aim for full abstinence or a gradual reduction. Live counters, mood and craving insights, an on-device coach, and tools for difficult moments. Everything stays on your phone.

Download on the App Store

What you get

Track what matters

Multiple trackers, abstinence or reduction, money saved, milestones, and a clean-days calendar — without shame when life gets hard.

Compassionate relapse

Log cravings and slips honestly. A lapse does not erase your progress — your best streak and lifetime stats stay with you.

Understand patterns

Heatmaps, triggers, mood trends, and journal analysis — all computed on-device to spot when and why risk peaks.

Coach on your iPhone

Apple Intelligence powers a private coaching chat (iOS 26+). Your context never leaves the device. Weekly recap included with Premium.

Crisis support

Guided breathing, emergency plan with a trusted contact, regional helplines — designed for the moment an urge hits.

Apple ecosystem

Home Screen widgets, Live Activity, Siri shortcuts, optional Screen Time blocking, and Health correlations — privacy-first.
